This course is for you if: You're a UX or product designer who wants to build real things, not just mock them up. You want to use Claude and Figma to go from idea to working website. You want to sit in a meeting with engineers and actually understand what they're saying.

You don't need to memorize syntax. You don't need to become a "developer." You need fluency ‚Äî the ability to read code, direct AI to write it, translate your designs into reality, and hand off work that developers actually love working with.
